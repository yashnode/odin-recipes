Paneer Tikka Masala

Description

This restaurant style Paneer Tikka Masala recipe is brimming with bright flavors from the spiced tomato onion curry sauce and delightfully marinated grilled cottage cheese. While this dish may be considered a labor of love, the time and effort are well worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 pound paneer
  • 2 onions
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir paneer until golden, about 5 minutes.
  2. Grind the onion, ginger, and garlic into a paste using a mortar and pestle.
  3. Mix tomato sauce, half-and-half, and salt into paneer mixture; simmer until thickened, about 30 minutes.
